CP1406/CP5638 - Assignment 1 - Project Plan

Name: Rishan Ratha

Goal

Build a clear, trustworthy website for 100SMILES Dental Care that increases new-patient bookings and email enquiries by 20% within 3 months. The site will make it easy to contact the clinic, understand services, and feel confident about the team—leading to more completed preventive check-ups and cleanings.

Success Evaluation

The site will be judged successful if, over the first 12 weeks:

  • Bookings: ≥20% increase in first-visit bookings (measured via email bookings and phone calls originating from the site).
  • Enquiries: ≥20% increase in contact emails compared to the previous 12 weeks.
  • Engagement: ≥60% of visitors view both “Our Team” and “Contact Us” pages in a session.
  • Usability: ≥80% positive feedback from a quick patient survey (clarity, trust, ease of finding contact info).

Target Audience

Primary audiences:

  • Families in Razole seeking routine dental care (check-ups/cleanings) and emergency support.
  • Working adults & seniors wanting reliable, friendly care and clear pricing/offers.

Design to serve this audience: Calm, clinical colours (soft blues/white) to signal hygiene and trust; large readable headings; simple 3-item navigation (Home, Our Team, Contact Us); prominent phone/email; photos that show compassionate care; concise copy with benefits and current offers.

Site Flowchart

The site is intentionally small and focused:

  • Home → overview, key services, offers, CTA to contact
  • Our Team → dentist profiles and roles to build trust
  • Contact Us → phone, email, address, hours, offers reminder
